Frequently Asked Questions

Can you see me today?

Yes, we make every effort to see emergency patients the same day they call. Call us at (818) 600-6866 and we'll find the fastest available appointment for you.

What if I don't have dental insurance?

We offer flexible payment options for patients without insurance. We'll work with you to find a payment plan that fits your budget. Call us to discuss your options.

Do you offer help for severe tooth pain at night or weekends?

Call us at (818) 600-6866 and we'll help you figure out the best next step. We'll assess your situation and guide you on whether you need immediate care or can wait until our next available appointment.

Will you just pull my tooth?

Our goal is always to save your natural tooth when possible. We'll explore all treatment options with you and only recommend extraction if it's truly necessary. We'll explain everything clearly so you can make an informed decision.

Is it better to go to the ER or a dentist for tooth pain?

For most dental emergencies, a dentist is the better choice. ERs can provide pain medication but can't treat the underlying dental problem. We can address the root cause and get you lasting relief. If you're experiencing severe swelling that affects breathing or swallowing, go to the ER immediately.

How much will my emergency visit cost?

Costs vary depending on your specific situation and treatment needs. We'll provide a clear estimate after your exam and X-ray, and we'll check your insurance benefits if you have coverage. Call us at (818) 600-6866 and we'll help you understand your options.
